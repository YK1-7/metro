Idea About Riyadh Metro

Public transportation in Riyadh was established under the leadership of His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, Crown Prince, Prime Minister, and Chairman of the Royal Commission for Riyadh City (RCRC). This comprehensive transportation system serves the residents and visitors of Riyadh The system connects the city through a variety of transport modes, including metro lines, buses, bus rapid transit (BRT), and feeder buses, providing seamless connectivity across the city. It also offers “Park & Ride” facilities for commuters Riyadh’s public transportation is dedicated to delivering high-quality, user-friendly services that contribute to economic growth and environmental sustainability. By meeting diverse mobility needs, the system enhances ease of movement within the city, reduces reliance on private vehicles, and makes traveling across the Saudi capital easy, reliable, and convenient
